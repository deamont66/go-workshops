So... what is it? (and isn't)

  • Object-oriented, not type-oriented
    • no inheritance, type hierarchy
    • methods on any type, but not classes or subclasses
  • Implicit (mostly), not explicit
    • types are often inferred, not declared
    • interfaces derived, not specified
  • Concurrent, not parallel
    • intended for program structure (simplicity), not maximum performance
